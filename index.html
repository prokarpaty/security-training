How to spot a phishing email next time

Check the sender's email domain carefully — attackers use look-alike domains (e.g. c-quilibrium.com)
Urgent language like "24 hours" or "act now" is a common red flag
Hover over links before clicking to verify the real destination URL
When in doubt, contact IT directly instead of clicking any link
Legitimate IT teams will never ask for your password via email
